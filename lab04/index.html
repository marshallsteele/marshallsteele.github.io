Meet The Team


Dr. Jennifer Prada, DVM
& Brittany

Having graduated from the Ontario Veterinary College at the University of Guelph and completed 5 years of training throughout Ontario, Jennifer joined our team in 2019. Jennifer was raised on the family farm and her love for animals grew from there. In her free time, she enjoys cycling and baking homemade bread.

Ryan, RVT
& Beans

Ryan has worked as a registered veterinary technician for 10 years across Canada. He graduated from Dalhousie University in Nova Scotia in 2010 and joined the Athos Veterinary team in 2014. When not working, Ryan loves to play baseball and go snowboarding with friends.

Rachel, VA
& Rucker

Rachel, our newest addition to the team, recieved her veterinary assistant certificate form Algonquin College in 2020. Rachel volunteered with Athos Veterinary throughout her program. Her passion for animals extends to her home life, as she loves taking care of her dog Rucker and her two horses, Lady and Fancy.
